Our Founders:

Rev. Sharon K. Jones

Minister Kirk Lomotey

Rev. Dr. Sharon K. Jones is CEO and Founding Shepherd of In His Hands Ministries. She is an anointed and spirit filled woman of God, who loves the Lord with all of her heart, mind and soul. Before planting In His Hands Ministries, Inc., Rev. Jones was a member of the First Baptist Church of North Brentwood, Maryland where she was groomed as a young child to nurture a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. At the age of 15, she was one of the first young people to be appointed as a junior Deaconess as the elders of the church saw the Spirit of the Lord upon her at a very early age. On April 12, 2000 she was licensed to preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ by the Reverend Dr. Perry A. Smith, III, Pastor of First Baptist Church of North Brentwood. Later, while attending seminary, Rev. Jones received additional ministerial training at Shiloh Baptist Church of Washington, DC where she was a very active member of the ministerial team assisting Reverend Dr. Wallace Charles Smith in ministering his God given vision of Building the Village by Caring.

Reverend Jones is a native of Prince Georgeâ€™s County, Maryland where she attended public schools and is a graduate of the University of Maryland where she was awarded a Bachelor of Science degree in Psychology with a specialized concentration in Behavioral and Social Science. She has a Master of Divinity degree from Howard University School of Divinity (summa cum laude), is ordained to the Gospel Ministry through the American Baptist Convention, USA and the DC Baptist Convention and is the recipient of the prestigious Rev. Dr. Earl Harrison Leadership award and the James Daniel and Brittie Tyms award which is presented by the faculty to the graduating senior who has conspicuously and consistently demonstrated the qualities necessary for an informed, compassionate ministry of nurture as a religious educator. Rev. Jones graduated from Wesley Theological Seminary in Washington, DC in 2016 completing her doctoral studies in Church Leadership and Excellence and is completing an MBA specializing in Global Business Management at the University of Maryland. Dr. Jones currently serves as the Director of Service, Outreach & Missions Ministries at Mt. Hope Baptist Church in Fredericksburg, VA under the leadership of the Reverend Dr. Raymond A. Bell, Jr. Dr. Jones has extensive experience in pastoral care and counseling, ministry development and leadership development.

Dr. Jones is married to Minister Kirk Lomotey, is the proud mother of two grown daughters, ShaNai Kristina and India Rayshon and the grandmother to Zahra and Octavian. She is a living testimony to Proverbs 22:6, Train a child in the way she should go and when she is old she will not depart from it.
