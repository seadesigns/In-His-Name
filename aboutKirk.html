Minister Kirk Lomotey

Minister Kirk Lomotey was born in Harlem, New York and attended the Borough of Manhattan Community College and the University of South Dakota where he majored in Computer Science. His parents were originally from Ghana West Africa and both had a major impact in raising him to be a compassionate advocate for the common man.

He accepted Jesus Christ as his Lord and Savior at the age of 23. He has facilitated several classes including the class titled, Equipping the Saints Discipleship Bible Study Class. He is an Adult Sunday School teacher and has served as the Ministry Leader of Research and Data Ministry team and is currently serving as the Ministry Leader for the Evangelism Ministry at Mt. Hope Baptist church. He also serves as a greeter, serves on the Audio Media ministry and truly believes that churches have a unique position in the world to share the good news and make a strong impact on social crisis issues. He loves people, serving in ministry and especially loves facilitating Discipleship Bible studies and providing customer service and leadership workshops. He is a trained and gifted facilitator who has coached and mentored managers of fortune 100 companies.

He is married to Reverend Sharon Jones and is the stepfather of two grown daughters ShaNai and India. He hopes to attend seminary this upcoming year.

His favorite scripture is Joshua 24:15 which states, But if serving the Lord seems undesirable to you, then choose for yourselves this day whom you will serve, whether the gods your ancestors served beyond the Euphrates, or the gods of the Amorites, in whose land you are living. But as for me and my household, we will serve the Lord.
