Services

Below is a list of some of the services that we provide.

Pastoral Referral

Assist candidates who desire to Pastor churches with building their Ministerial resume and packet for pulpit vacancies throughout the world.

Ministerial Staffing

We are also a Ministerial staffing company to provide the Universal Church with qualified and highly trained Ministers.

Ministry Development Consulation

Whether it is sprucing up an already established ministry or creating a new one from the ground up…we are here to help.

Ministeral Training

We give ministers and would-be ministers the training needed to improve their skill and gift in order for them to be effective leaders.

Pastoral Support

Provide support to church pastors whenever needed which include but are not limited to staffing and ministry consultation.

Church Outreach Consultation

We help churches develop an outreach programs that helps reach the hearts and minds of those within the church’s communities.

Additional Services Offered:

  • Sermon Development Training
  • Sick/shut-in Visitations
  • Communion
  • Pulpit Etiquette
  • Preaching and Teaching Engagements
  • Workshops and Seminars
