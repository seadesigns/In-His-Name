The "Core" consists of the processes that we use in order to train and assign pastoral and ministerial staff. It also includes a list of the basic requirements in order for someone to be include within our staffing database.

  • Spiritual Gift Assessment will be given to determine the candidate's leadership gift mixing.
  • An understanding of the degree of commitment that is needed to lead.
  • Need little supervision and can delegate through supervision.
  • Can empower others and can think independently and critically.
  • Is an effective communicator and have strong people skills.
  • Personality Assessment will be given.
  • A Leadership Application to be filled out by the applicant.
  • Write a two page Consideration Statement as to why you believe you have leadership qualities and why you are considering leading a ministry
  • Persons can be nominated to join the Core by another leader.
  • All applicants must be approved by the Pastor.
  • Interviews will be given to all approved and qualified applicants.
  • A database of leaders will be built and given to the Church.
  • Applicants should be involved in a ministry for at least 6 months.
  • Attend Bible Study or Sunday School.
