About Us:

In His Hands Ministries was originally founded by Rev. Dr. Sharon K. Jones in 2005 and was later redeveloped into the ministry it is today. Along with her husband Minister Kirk Lomotey, they have developed a ministry that not only helps churches create effective and efficient ministries, but they also specialize in creating church leaders who can inspire, lead and motivate the people of God. Additionally, we also serve as a staffing ministry that provide churches with the best fit for their teams whether they are searching for pastors, or other leaders of support. IHHM provides training and leadership development to clergy who would like to sharpen their ministerial gifts and skills.

Goals and Objectives:

  • To provide strong, intentional and effective leadership in the Church.
  • To maximize adaptive leadership potential.
  • To build a Core of Leaders who are gifted, well trained, fully developed and ready for assignment whenever vacancies occur.
  • To provide training and leadership development

Mission Statement:

To advance the Kingdom of God by developing transformational leaders in the church who inspire and develop ministry teams that transform the world for Jesus Christ.
