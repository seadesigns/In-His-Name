About Us:

In His Hands Ministries is a small family owned business that was originally founded by Rev Sharon Jones and Minister Kirk Lomotey. Together they have created a company that not only helps churches regardless of their chosen denomination create effective and efficient ministries they also specialize in creating church leaders who can inspire, lead and motivate their flocks. We also serve as a small staffing firm that helps churches choose the best fit for their team whether the be pastors, church custodians or pastoral supporting staff members.

Goals and Objectives:

  • To provide strong, intentional and effective leadership in the ministries of the Church.
  • To maximize adaptive leadership potential.
  • To build a Core of Leaders who are well trained, fully developed and ready for assignment whenever vacancies occur.

Mission Statement:

To build treansformational leaders in the church who inspire and develop ministry teams that transform the world for Christ.
